RENEW PALTZ

VIEW APP PROTOTYPE

ReNew Paltz is a concept for a marketplace app that focuses on buying and selling used school supplies within the SUNY New Paltz community. It was created and designed by a group of classmates and I with the goal to reduce waste and help students save money. We wanted it to have a simple user interface to make it easy for students to use and navigate. To achieve this, we did a lot of UX/UI research before beginning the final design.

Caitlin McGetrick, Designer
