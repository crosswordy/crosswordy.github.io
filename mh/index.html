MADELINE HALL

CASE STUDY PDF

Madeline Hall is a concept for a restaurant. Located in Gardiner, NY, it is a large venue suited for an intimate night out, a small get together, or any big event with those you love. It has a chic contemporary feel with an eclectic vintage flair, and the atmosphere is mellow and relaxing, serving American-fare and unique cocktails. I created the branding suite to reflect this feeling, and it features a muted color palette and monoline sans serif fonts.
