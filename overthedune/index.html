OVER THE DUNE

Over the Dune is a logo design that a client reached out to create for their beach house. The name of their house was “Over the Dune,” and they wanted a logo that they could put on a sign out front and use on other items as well. The colors used reflect the theme of their house, and the moon icon that replaces the letter D is a reference to a saying they like to use in their family: “I love you to the moon and beyond”.

Caitlin McGetrick, Graphic Designer
