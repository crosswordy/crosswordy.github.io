THE POWER OF PRIVACY

LINK TO SITE

The Power of Privacy: an Introduction to Mobile Data Collection is the culmination of my year long graphic design senior thesis project at SUNY New Paltz. I designed and coded a mobile-oriented site about data privacy in relation to the apps on our cellphones. Because of the technical and possibly intimidating nature of the topic, I wanted the visual language of the project to be engaging and approachable. It is formatted into three articles that answer the questions “how does my cellphone collect data from me?”, “why does it matter?”, and “how can I prevent it from happening?”. I was able to take the content I designed for mobile view and rework it into a physical zine, and I have future plans to use it in video format as well.

Caitlin McGetrick, Designer
